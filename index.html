Portfolio

Sentiment Analysis for SEC 10K Reports

Leveraging natural language processing techniques such as text extraction, stop word removal, tokenization and word count, this project compares the sentiment of fiscal year 2021 10-k financial reports for five pharmaceutical companies: Pfizer Inc., Moderna, Inc., Johnson & Johnson, Eli Lilly and Company and AbbVie Inc, based on text in items 1A. Risk Factors and 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations. The output includes a quantitative comparison of sentiment word frequency and lists of top ten sentiment words that most frequently appeared for each company.

Employment Growth and Office Property Values

This project assesses the impact of various job growth metrics on office property values from 2010 to 2020, using data from Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) and National Council of Real Estate Investment Fiduciaries (NCREIF) Property Index. Model with the lowest root mean square error (RMSE), Random Forest, achieved ~70% R squared score.
